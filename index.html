FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Common questions about our educational gaming systems

What age groups are your games suitable for?

+

Our games are designed for students from grades 6-12 (ages 11-18), with difficulty levels that can be adjusted by educators to match curriculum requirements and student abilities.

How historically accurate are the game environments?

+

We work with historians and educational consultants to ensure high levels of historical accuracy. Our environments, characters, and scenarios are based on extensive research and primary sources whenever possible.

What technical requirements do schools need?

+

Our packages include all necessary hardware and software. Schools only need to provide adequate space, standard power outlets, and internet connectivity. Our team handles all installation and setup.

How do teachers integrate this into their curriculum?

+

Each historical scenario comes with comprehensive lesson plans, pre/post activities, discussion guides, and assessment tools aligned with educational standards. Our professional development training helps teachers maximize the educational value.

What historical periods do your games cover?

+

Our current library includes Ancient Civilizations (Egypt, Greece, Rome), Medieval Europe, Renaissance, Age of Exploration, American Revolution, Civil War, World Wars, and more. We regularly add new content based on educator feedback.

How do you measure educational outcomes?

+

Our teacher dashboard provides detailed analytics on student engagement, progress, and assessment results. We also conduct regular research studies with partner schools to measure knowledge retention and critical thinking development.
